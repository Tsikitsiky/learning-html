Tips for writing HTML

How to write HTML quicker?

These are some tips to make your writing quicker:

How do you make sure that your work is high-quality?

To make sure that your work is high-quality, you should:
  1. make sure that your indenting is correct so that it is easier for people to read it
  2. make sure that your use of English(grammar and spelling) is correct
  3. validate your work to a validator in order to aware you in case of error on using the elements or the attributes
